Space

"Space is disease and danger wrapped in darkness and silence" Someone on Star Trek said that. I've never actually seen Star Trek, never possessed a dream of becoming an astronaut, but there are times when I find myself pondering space. A lifelong New Yorker, I only rarely get to see the stars, and when I do they are just faint pinpricks of light against the polluted sky. And then I look at pictures of space and am reminded just how vast and colorful the universe is. It's pretty breathtaking.
